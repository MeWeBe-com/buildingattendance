Emergency procedure

RED - Significant compliance issues which if left unchecked will have an adverse effect on the health and safety performance / management of the site. These health and safety issues should be given immediate attention and actions should be implemented within the agreed timescale.
(Example: Breach of Statutory Obligations or systematic failure of system management system).

MEDIUM - Less significant issues but still may pose a health and safety or business risk to Morgan Stanley.
(Example : Failure to meet documented Policies and Procedures or highlighted low compliance scoring by audit mechanisms).

GREEN - Good overall compliance of the agreed safety management system indicators.
Example : High Compliance scoring by audit mechanisms).
